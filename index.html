Mission Statement

Our mission is two-fold. First, we strive to transform K-12 education by offering free and innovative tutoring through hands-on learning experiences in a variety of academic fields (with a focus on the Medical Sciences, Literature, and Computer Science). Second, we aspire to advocate for current issues in these fields by developing and leading projects in both our immediate community and worldwide.
